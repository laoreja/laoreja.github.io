XIUYE GU

I'm a master student at Computer Science Department, Stanford University. Previously, I obtained my B.E. in Computer Science at Zhejiang University, where I was an undergraduate member of State Key Lab of CAD & CG, under the supervision of Prof. Deng Cai. I also spent half a year (9/2018-3/2019) and summer 2016 working under the guidance of Prof. Yong Jae Lee as a visiting research scholar, at UC Davis.

My primary research interest is computer vision. More specifically, I'm interested in improving machines' visual understanding through taking multi-modal inputs and better modeling.
