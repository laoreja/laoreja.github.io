XIUYE GU

I'm a master student majoring in Computer Science at Stanford University. Previously, I obtained my B.E. in Computer Science at Zhejiang University, where I worked with Prof. Deng Cai on content based image retrieval and computer vision. I also spent half a year (9/2018-3/2019) and summer 2016 working with Prof. Yong Jae Lee on computer vision as a visiting research scholar, at UC Davis. In summer 2018, I interned at TuSimple and worked on 3D point cloud scene flow estimation with Dr. Panqu Wang.

My primary research interest is computer vision. More specifically, I'm interested in improving machines' visual understanding through taking multi-modal inputs and better modeling.
